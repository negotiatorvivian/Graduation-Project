West Edmonton Mall

The West Edmonton Mall is located in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada and is the largest shopping mall in North America and the fifth largest in the world. There are more than 800 shops, a variety of dining and snack bars, 3D amusement parks, golf courses, water parks, sea lion shows, pet zoos, shooting ranges, hotels, cinemas, radio stations, etc.
